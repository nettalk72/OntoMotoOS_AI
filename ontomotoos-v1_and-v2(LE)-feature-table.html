Summary:
  • v1.x: Focused on single-system stability, basic self-audit, and formalized but static evolution. Simpler, easier to deploy, ideal for basic study or as a formal reference model.

  • v2.x (LE): Quantum/multiverse mesh, adaptive feedback, lightweight but always-on audit, mesh/creative sandbox, much more dynamic and resilient — ideal for simulation, research, or teaching adaptive/collective systems.
  • Kairos-Metis (2.1–2.2 LE): Adds advanced creative and audit layers (sandbox, quota, explainability) for "What if?" research and safe creative exploration.
